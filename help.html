JOURNEY THROUGH THE SOLAR SYSTEM

TEACHER ASSISTANCE

This WebQuest is intended to introduce students from the 5th/6th grade to the defining characteristics of our Solar System's planets. Some basic introduction to this topic might be needed, as the WebQuest uses terms generally specific to space exploration (i.e. satellites).

The activity is meant for classes of 16 to 24 students. Ideally, the class should be divided into 8 groups of 2-3 students in order to have each group make a poster about a different planet, but this structure may be changed to accommodate the teacher's/students' needs. The students are expected to complete the two tasks in about a week.

You may suggest any changes to the WebQuest by contacting me through any of the methods below.
